# Asset Download List — The Chronicles of Zoal

> Place files exactly as noted. None of these block coding — they slot into
> placeholders without logic changes. **Free up disk space first (C: is ~99% full).**

## ⚡ Fastest path (grab these packs first — they cover many items at once)
- **Quaternius — free CC0 packs** (trees, rocks, weapons, monsters): https://quaternius.com/
- **Kenney — free CC0 game assets** (props, particles, UI): https://kenney.nl/assets
- **Mixamo** (enemy characters + animations, free w/ Adobe login): https://www.mixamo.com/
- **Poly Haven** (HDR skies for skyboxes, CC0): https://polyhaven.com/hdris/skies
- Sketchfab free models hub: https://sketchfab.com/features/free-3d-models

Tip: a Quaternius monster/weapon pack alone can satisfy most enemy + weapon slots.
